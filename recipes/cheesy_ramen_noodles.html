Cheesy Ramen Noodles

Description

A tasty spin on typical ramen noodles, simply made by mixing in cheese!

Extremely cost efficient but not very nutritionally beneficial, only containing:

  • Calories: 163
  • Fat: 11g
  • Carbs: 8g
  • Protein: 8g

Note: This recipe is easily modifiable to include different ingredients if desired!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 package of ramen noodles (any flavour)
  • 1 slice of American cheese

Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan
  2. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender (approx. 2 minutes)
  3. Pour out water and stir in seasoning packet and cheese until well blended
  4. Serve hot
